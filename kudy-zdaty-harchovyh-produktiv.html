Redirecting...

This guide page covers the topic 'Redirecting...' and keeps a short raw-HTML summary available without JavaScript.

For 'Redirecting...', the important question is not only where the batch goes next, but also what preparation, storage and handover conditions are expected before collection.

Use this page as a short guide checkpoint for 'Redirecting...': identify the stream, verify how the batch is prepared, and then move to the linked guide sections for logistics, documents, sorting, recycling or final disposal.
